Our Story

Jayme and Daniella first met in college (GO TERPS!) when he was pledging a frat that Daniella's friends were in. One typical class-skipping Thursday (sorry mom and dad), Daniella and some of her friends decided to play drinking games at their apartment, "Cocktail Lounge." The boys (we won't tell you who to save them from embarassment, but some of them are groomsmen) inevitably trashed the beloved Cocktail Lounge. The girls had nothing to do with the mess, obviously.

The boys graciously offered to clean the apartment by sacrificing one of their pledge's to come and do their job. So who did they end up sending? None other than "Jaymo Dollas." Not only did this kid have to clean a disgusting apartment, but he was told he had to "throw an original pick-up line" at each girl.

Well, just as you suspected, Jaymo KILLED it with the pick-up lines. Daniella and Jaymo became good friends the rest of that year, and it wasn't until that summer where they started seeing their friendship as something more.

And the rest is history.

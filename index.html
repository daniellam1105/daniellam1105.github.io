Our Story

Jayme and Daniella first met in college (GO TERPS!) while Daniella was living in the infamous "Cocktail Lounge" apartment and Jayme was pledging the Delta Chi fraternity. One typical class-skipping Thursday (sorry mom and dad), Daniella and some of her friends decided to play drinking games at "Cocktail Lounge." The boys (we won't tell you who to save them from embarassment, Liguori & Christman) inevitably "trashed" the beloved Cocktail Lounge. The girls had nothing to do with the mess, obviously...

The boys graciously sacrificed one of their pledges to clean the apartment. So who did they end up sending? None other than "Jaymo Dollas." Not only did he have to clean a disgusting apartment, but he was told he had to "throw an original pick-up line" at each girl.

Well, just as you suspected, Jaymo KILLED it with the pick-up lines. Daniella and Jaymo got closer and closer as the year went on, before ultimately falling in love during the summer of 2010.

And the rest is history.
